Rules & Regulations

Read carefully before participating — violations result in disqualification

🎮 Eligibility
  • Players must be 13 years of age or older to participate.
  • Each player may only register for one team per tournament.
  • Players must own a legitimate, unmodified copy of the game.
  • Competitors must be available for all scheduled match times.
  • Banned or suspended accounts are ineligible to compete.
⚙ Technical Requirements
  • A stable internet connection is required — disconnects do not pause matches.
  • Players must use the region's designated servers unless otherwise specified.
  • All hardware and peripherals must be standard consumer-grade equipment.
  • Third-party software, macros, or scripts are strictly prohibited.
  • Minimum ping requirement: under 200ms to the match server.
🏆 Match Rules
  • Teams must be ready 15 minutes before scheduled start time.
  • A 10-minute grace period is granted — forfeit after that.
  • Match settings must follow the official competition config.
  • All matches are best-of-3; finals are best-of-5.
  • Screenshot or video proof is required for disputed matches.
  • Reschedules require mutual agreement 24 hours in advance.
🚫 Prohibited Conduct
  • Cheating, exploiting, hacking, or using third-party tools.
  • Smurfing — using alternate or boosted accounts.
  • Harassment, hate speech, or abusive communication.
  • Collusion, match-fixing, or intentional losing.
  • Sharing or streaming match footage before results are official.
⚖ Dispute Resolution
  • Disputes must be filed within 15 minutes of match completion.
  • Evidence must be submitted via the official dispute portal.
  • Tournament admins' decisions are final and binding.
  • Frivolous disputes may result in a warning or penalty.
  • Escalated disputes may be reviewed by the head admin panel.
💸 Prize Distribution
  • Prizes are distributed within 14 business days of tournament end.
  • Winners must submit valid ID and payment details to claim prizes.
  • Prize winnings may be subject to local tax laws.
  • Disqualified players or teams forfeit all prize eligibility.
  • Cash prizes are transferred via PayPal, bank transfer, or crypto.
📜 By registering, all participants agree to abide by these rules and any amendments published before the tournament begins. Nexus Arena reserves the right to update regulations at any time.
